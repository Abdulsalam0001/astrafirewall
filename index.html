ABOUT TAYO CONGA

  • MY PHILOSOPHY
  • VISION
  • MY ENGAGEMENTS

Tayo Conga has an energtic and African Aura

HAPPINESS – To put smiles on people's faces at all times through the power of the 'Conga'.

Tayo Conga aims to spotlight and propagate the African Traditional music using percussion.

He also aims to infuse the current West African Sounds (Afropop) with the African Traditional music (percussion).

He strives to further spotlight the African Traditional sounds (percussion) on the world map.

Tayo Conga has at different times worked with various Nigerian 'A-list' artistes of gospel and secular music. Some of these include Flavour, Wizkid, Banky W, Nathaniel Bassey, Sammie Okposo, Debo Ojubuyi (Zion Ilaje) among others. He has also featured at the back end of several music video productions and live concerts.
